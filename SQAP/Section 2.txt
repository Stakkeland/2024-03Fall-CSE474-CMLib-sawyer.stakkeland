Section 2
Definitions and Acronyms

'Call of Duty' | A first person shooter video game that has annual releases
Legacy title | A Call of Duty title that is not the current release
8's Lobby | A private lobby that requires users to be invited to, this allows users to play competitive
            settings on legacy titles
Discord Bot | A application that can be added to a users either private or public server discord server.
              These bots can do anything and can be added to servers to add functionality that otherwise
             could not be had on discord
